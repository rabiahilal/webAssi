Gotcha: All-in-One Student Productivity App

Gotcha is a comprehensive productivity application designed to meet all student needs. The app offers a combination of tools, including a calendar, task manager, and AI-powered utilities for document summarization and quiz generation. It enhances productivity and streamlines student life with an intuitive interface and powerful features.

  • Technologies Used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Features:
    • Integrated Calendar and Task Manager
    • AI Tools for Document Summarization
    • Quiz Generation
    • Public Work Sharing Platform
